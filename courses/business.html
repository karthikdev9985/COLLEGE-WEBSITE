Business Analysis Courses

  • Customer Analysis
    • Customer analytics is a process by which data from customer behavior is used to help make key business decisions via market segmentation and predictive analytics. This information is used by businesses for direct marketing, site selection, and customer relationship management.
  • Operation Analysis
    • Operational analytics is about using real-time data for daily decisions. Relevant business information flows from many sources into tools that analyze that data and identify problems and opportunities. This actionable data is then used by teams to inform their decision-making.
  • People Analysis
